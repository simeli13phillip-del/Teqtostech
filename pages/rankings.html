4 Professional Levels

Progress through certifications to unlock better opportunities

🟢

Level 1

Beginner Technician

Who they are:

New to AV/freelancing, basic installations, support roles

Requirements:
  • • No certification OR entry-level certs
  • • Basic training from AVIXA
  • • Entry courses from Extron
Platform Access:
  • • Small/local jobs
  • • Assistant roles
🔵

Level 2

Certified Technician

Who they are:

Skilled and employable internationally

Requirements (ANY 1-2):
  • • CTS from AVIXA
  • • Network+ from CompTIA
  • • Dante Level 2 from Dante
Platform Access:
  • • Standard freelance jobs
  • • Remote contracts
  • • Verified badge
🟣

Level 3

Advanced Specialist

Who they are:

Highly skilled technicians with specialization

Requirements (ANY 2-3):
  • • CTS-I or CTS-D
  • • CCNA from Cisco
  • • Crestron/Extron certs
  • • Biamp/Shure advanced certs
Platform Access:
  • • High-paying gigs
  • • International contracts
  • • System integration roles
🔴

Level 4

Elite Technician

Who they are:

Industry-level experts

Requirements (STRICT):
  • • CTS + specialization
  • • Strong networking (CCNA+)
  • • High-level manufacturer cert
  • • Proven experience + reviews
Platform Access:
  • • Premium/global jobs
  • • Direct hiring by companies
  • • Featured profiles
  • • Highest earnings
